What is the drone flight time calculator?
The drone flight time calculator is a tool that computes the period that the drone will be flying for.
How to calculate drone flight time?
To calculate drone flight time you should use the following formula:
drone flight time = (battery capacity * (battery discharge / 100) / (all up weight * 170 / battery voltage)) * 60
